Some people have a type in dogs. I have three. And honestly? Each one is more fabulous than the last. From spotted socialites to cream-colored royalty to sleek runway models — here is my definitive love letter to the world's most iconic dog breeds.

The original it-girl 🐾

The Dalmatian

Bold, spotted, and born famous. Dalmatians are the supermodels of the dog world — and they know it. Every single one walks like they own the sidewalk.

Why Dalmatians Steal the Show

  1. The Look
    • No two Dalmatians have the same spot pattern — each one is completely unique
    • Born completely white — the spots appear over time like a slow reveal
    • That black and white contrast is literally iconic
  2. The Personality
    • Outgoing, playful, and endlessly social
    • Always ready for a walk, a run, or a photoshoot
    • Deeply loyal to their people
  3. The Cultural Moment
    • 101 Dalmatians? Still iconic.
    • Fire station mascots since the 1800s

More Dalmatian Moments 🐾

English Cream
Mini Dachshund

Long-haired, cream-colored, and completely unaware of how small they are. Pure elegance in the most compact package.

Why They're Irresistible

  1. That Coat
    • Long, feathery fur that flows when they walk — very dramatic, very correct
    • English cream coloring is the rarest and most sought-after variety
    • Gets silkier and more beautiful as they age
  2. The Personality
    • Stubborn but so charming you forgive them immediately
    • Convinced they are a large dog in disguise
    • Champion cuddlers — they will be on your lap constantly
  3. The Aesthetic
    • Cream fur looks incredible against a pink bow or bandana
    • Tiny little legs that somehow still strut
Cream & dreamy ✨
Sleek, chic, iconic 💎

Italian Greyhound

The supermodel of the dog world. Sleek, slender, and impossibly elegant — tan and white Italian Greyhounds look like they were designed by a fashion house.

The Iggy Experience

  1. The Silhouette
    • Arched back, tucked waist, long graceful neck — an actual work of art
    • Tan and white coloring is warm, soft, and utterly photogenic
    • Their little paws are perfectly dainty
  2. The Personality
    • Dramatic — will refuse to walk in the rain
    • Velcro dogs: they want to be glued to you at all times
    • Surprisingly fast — they are sighthounds after all
  3. The Fashion Potential
    • They need sweaters due to their thin coat — built-in excuse to accessorize
    • A pink harness on a tan iggy? Unmatched visual.
